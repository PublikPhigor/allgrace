About Our Company

All Grace Energy Limited is an integrated oil and gas company and the Operator of the Ubima Marginal Field (in OML 17) Rivers State Nigeria. All Grace Energy Limited is developing the Ubima Marginal Field as a mechanism for creating localized domestic market using Small Scale Gas Utilization Projects as the backbone and an integral part of all field development activities. With this unique scheme, All Grace Energy Limited will operate the Ubima field as a zero-gas flare facility, an objective that is in tandem with Government policies on local content and community development.
