What does Sustainability mean to All Grace Energy Limited?

Sustainability to All Grace Energy Limited means clean, safe and reliable energy is provided to the local domestic market. Also, All Grace Energy Limited ensures that our business activities do not in any way jeopardise the environment. We take precaution when undertaking business projects by assessing their environmental risks and impacts as well as any health hazards.

1. Health, Safety, Security, Environment and Social Performance: AGEL gives top priority to Health, Safety, Security and the Environment when making business decisions. A major contributing factor to our long-term business success is the commitment of all our personnel to a strong HSE culture. All Grace has in place all Health, Safety and Environment (HSE) system documentation. We are committed to the prevention of accidents, incidents and spills through the provision of healthy, safe and stable working conditions for our personnel on and at all our premises. AGEL commits to training and re-training of its staff in all HSE standards.

2. Corporate Social Responsibility: All Grace Energy Limited works with diverse local organisations within our community and in our places of operations. We know the importance of education and as such provide scholarships and funding to secondary schools and undergraduate students. We work with NGOs such as project one Nigeria on providing clothes for babies, funds for the girl-child movement and children who have been molested.

All Grace also assists graduates who wants to start up a business or who have started a business and need funding to keep the business afloat. We also support the local health care centres in our community. All Grace Energy Limited contributes to the economic growth of the country by providing jobs to the communities in which we carry out our operation.
